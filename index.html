Sticker Specifications

  • Printed on glossy printable vinyl (WeLiu)
  • Laminated with holographic vinyl laminate (Outus) for water-resistance and scratch protection
  • Stickers are water-resistant (not waterproof)
  • Best for laptops, notebooks, phone cases, water bottles (light use), and indoor surfaces
  • Cut using Silhouette Cameo 4 — clean die-cut edges
  • Not intended for long-term outdoor exposure

Additional Information

  • Intricate die-cut shapes: May include a small setup fee: +$0.10 – $0.20 per sticker or $10 flat depending on complexity
  • Rush orders available: $15 – $30 depending on turnaround time
