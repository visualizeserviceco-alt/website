Client Resources

Everything you need to know about working with Visualize Studio.

Project Process

1. Discovery & Planning

We start with understanding your brand, goals, and target audience. This phase includes research, competitor analysis, and project planning.

2. Design Phase

Our creative team develops initial concepts and designs based on your requirements. You'll receive drafts for review and feedback.

3. Development & Implementation

Once designs are approved, we move to development. For web projects, this includes coding, testing, and optimization.

4. Review & Launch

Final review, testing, and launch. We provide training and support to ensure you're comfortable with your new assets.

Payment Process

Payment Schedule

• 50% deposit required to start project
• 25% at design approval
• 25% upon project completion

Payment Methods

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. Invoices are sent via email with secure payment links.

Late Payments

Payments are due within 15 days of invoice date. Late payments may result in project delays or suspension.

Terms of Service

Project Scope

Project scope is defined in your project agreement. Additional work outside the scope will be quoted separately.

Revisions

Each project includes up to 3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are charged at our hourly rate.

Intellectual Property

Upon final payment, all intellectual property rights transfer to the client. We retain the right to showcase work in our portfolio.

Support & Warranty

We provide 30 days of support after project completion. This includes bug fixes and minor adjustments.
