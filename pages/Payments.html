Payment Structure

Clear and transparent payment terms for all projects.

Initial Payment

At least 50% of the total project cost is required to begin work. Projects will not start until this initial payment is received and confirmed.

Project Completion

Work progresses based on your feedback and revisions. You'll receive updates throughout the design process.

Final Payment & Delivery

100% of the payment must be received before any final files, deliverables, or completed work will be released. Once full payment is confirmed, you'll receive all project files in the agreed formats.
