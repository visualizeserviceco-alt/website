This Is How It Happens.

A streamlined 6-step process designed for speed and clarity.

01

Form & Brief

You'll fill out a short form to help me understand your project goals, target audience, and expectations—the foundation of the process.

02

Visual References & Moodboard

Share inspirations, ideas, or references. I'll create a moodboard or direction plan that captures the tone, style, and vision of your project.

03

Concept & Design

Based on the brief and references, I develop a clear concept—whether that's a brand identity, website, or creative project layout.

04

Identity System & Project Assets

I build the full system or deliverables your project requires—this could be brand guidelines, websites, digital assets, or print-ready files.

05

Feedback & Revisions

Your feedback guides the refinements—within your revision limit—until the project feels just right.

06

Final Delivery & Support

Final files or completed deliverables are shared in the proper formats once 100% of the payment has been received. Plus, I'm here to support you if you need help after launch.

Projects will not begin until an initial payment is made. The initial payment must be at least 50% of the total project cost. No final files, deliverables, or completed work will be released until 100% of the payment has been received.

FAQ

01 - How many revision rounds do I get?

Between 1 to 3 revisions depending on your package.

02 - How does payment work?

Projects will not begin until an initial payment is made. The initial payment must be at least 50% of the total project cost. No final files, deliverables, or completed work will be released until 100% of the payment has been received.

All designs are custom-crafted—no templates or stock assets.

04 - What file formats will I receive?

PNG, JPEG, SVG, and PDF.

05 - Do you offer post-project support?

Yes—feel free to reach out anytime after delivery for help.

06 - Can I use the designs on various platforms?

Absolutely—use them freely across websites, print, social media, and merchandise.

07 - Unsatisfied with the first concept?

I'll work with you through feedback and revisions until you're happy—your satisfaction is a priority.
